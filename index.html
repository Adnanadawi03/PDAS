Threat Classification

3 Levels of Risk

Every scan results in a clear, actionable risk classification so you always know what you're dealing with.

LOW RISK

Safe to Open

No significant phishing indicators found. The email passes all safety checks and is considered trustworthy.

MEDIUM RISK

Proceed with Caution

Some suspicious patterns detected. The email may contain misleading links or spoofed sender information.

HIGH RISK

Dangerous — Block

Clear phishing indicators found. This email is likely a coordinated attack and should be blocked immediately.
